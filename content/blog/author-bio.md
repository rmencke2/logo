# Rasmus Mencke - Executive Bio

Rasmus Mencke is a product and technology executive, operator, and advisor with a career spanning global enterprise platforms and high-growth scale-ups. He currently serves as defacto CEO and has primarily held CPTO and CPDO leadership roles across public, private, and PE-backed companies.

Over the past two decades, Rasmus has led product, engineering, and technology organizations at companies including Salesforce, Oracle, Planday, Bambuser, and Webnode. His experience covers the full company lifecycle, from growth acceleration and AI transformation to capital raises, M&A activity, and company scaling across international markets.

At Salesforce, he helped drive product strategy and go-to-market execution for Commerce Cloud in EMEA, contributing to significant revenue growth and enterprise expansion. At Planday, he played a key role in scaling the product organization, introducing AI-led initiatives, supporting a major growth round, and contributing to the path toward acquisition. At Bambuser and Webnode, he has led broad product and technology transformations with a focus on performance, innovation, and operational clarity.

Rasmus is known for connecting strategy to execution: aligning product, technology, commercial priorities, and leadership systems so organizations can move faster and make better decisions. His work today focuses on AI adoption with measurable business impact, scaling high-performing global teams, and helping leadership teams operate effectively through complexity and change.

Core themes in his writing and advisory work include:
- AI as an operating model, not a side project
- Leadership systems for scaling teams and decision quality
- Product and technology strategy tied to GTM and growth
- Executive execution across public, private, and PE-backed contexts
